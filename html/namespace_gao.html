◆ State

enum class Gao::State
strong

Possible states a Gaolette may present itself.

Allows Gao to restrict and place constraints on what the user may do to a Gaolette at any given time.

Enumerator
Operational 

the Gaolette is open for read/write

ShutDown 

the Gaolette is closed for read/write

Locked 

the Gaolette is locked and is read-only

Operating 

the Gaolette is running code and is open for read/write for console

Illformed 

the Gaolette is ill-formed and cannot be used
